Introduction

[BODY]
• App Name: PubConv - A real-time global chat experience.
• What it is: A lightweight, high-speed communication tool bridging web and mobile.
• Who it is for: Student collectives, hobbyist groups, and communities wanting instant, hassle-free interaction.

--------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 2
--------------------------------------------------
[TITLE]
Problem Statement

[BODY]
• Real Issue: Modern chat apps are bloated with unnecessary features, making them slow and chaotic.
• Real Issue: Maintaining safety from spam in public chat rooms is technically difficult for small teams.
• Who Experiences It: Community managers and users struggling with clunky app syncs when switching from desktop to mobile.

--------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 3
--------------------------------------------------
[TITLE]
Key Features

[BODY]
• Real-Time Interactivity: Instant messaging, @username tagging, and emoji reactions.
• Cross-Platform Delivery: Built natively for Web and seamlessly packaged for Android.
• Powerful Admin Controls: Dynamic chat rate-limiting, message deletion, and system-wide maintenance toggles.
• Integrated Community Tools: Built-in calendar events, user search, and customizable user profiles.

--------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 4
--------------------------------------------------
[TITLE]
How It Works

[BODY]
• Step 1 (Entry): Users sign in securely and enter the central global chat room.
• Step 2 (Connect): Users instantly drop into the conversation, seeing live online counts.
• Step 3 (Engage): Users tag others via the smart @ menu or reply directly to older messages.
• Step 4 (Moderate): Admins safely manage the flow by clearing spam or pausing chat in real-time.
