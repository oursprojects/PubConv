## Slide 2: Problem Statement
**Slide Text:**
- **The Problem:** Modern chat apps are bloated, fragmented, and heavy.
- **The Struggle:** Switching between Web and Mobile experiences is rarely seamless.
- **The Impact:** Communities face friction when trying to connect in real-time.

**Speaker Script:**
> "Let’s talk about the problem we set out to solve. If you look at many chat applications today, they are overloaded with features most people don’t need, making them slow and chaotic. Worse, they often enforce closed ecosystems where the experience moving from your laptop browser to your mobile phone is clunky. Community managers and everyday users suffer from this because setting up a truly real-time, easily moderated communication space shouldn't require complex setups. PubConv removes that friction, providing a unified space where people can talk globally, instantly, and without the bloat."

## Slide 3: Key Features
**Slide Text:**
- **Real-Time Communication:** Instant messaging with live online counts powered by WebSockets.
- **Rich Interactivity:** `@` mentions, specific message replies, and instant emoji reactions.
- **Robust Admin Moderation:** Spam rate limiting and one-click 'Clear Chat' capabilities.
- **Cross-Platform Delivery:** Works flawlessly on Web and native Android devices.

**Speaker Script:**
> "We didn't want to build just another messaging app; we wanted to focus on what makes a chat room strong. First, **Real-Time Communication**: We've implemented top-tier WebSocket technology so messages, typing indicators, and online user connections happen instantly. Second, we added **Rich Interactivity**: You can tag someone specifically using the '@' symbol, reply directly to an older message to keep conversations organized, and drop quick emoji reactions. Third, recognizing that public spaces need safety, we built **Powerful Admin Controls** including rate limiting to prevent spam and instant message deletion. And finally, it's a **Cross-Platform** app—meaning it runs just as beautifully natively on an Android device as it does in your web browser."

---

## Slide 4: How It Works
**Slide Text:**
- **1. Connect:** Open the app and instantly enter the global chat.
- **2. Engage:** Type your message or use `@` to search for active users.
- **3. React:** Tap any message to reply directly or leave an emoji.
- **4. Moderate:** Admins monitor and remove toxic content in real-time.

**Speaker Script:**
> "So, how does it work from a user's perspective? It’s wonderfully simple. Step 1: Open the app. There are no complicated servers to join—you are instantly in the Global Chat Room, and you immediately see who is online. Step 2: Start chatting. As you type, you can use the '@' symbol, which brings up a smart-search menu to tag someone specifically. Step 3: Interaction. By tapping and holding, or hovering over a message, you get quick menus to reply or react with an emoji. Finally, Step 4: Behind the scenes, administrators have unique badges and moderation tools seamlessly integrated directly into the chat interface, keeping the environment safe with just a click."
